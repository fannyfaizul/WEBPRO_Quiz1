Jembatan Sosrodilogo

The bridge named Sosrodilogo began to be built in 2016, when Bojonegoro was led by Dr. Suyoto, M.Sc., who is usually called Kang Yoto. The bridge was recently completed and inaugurated by Kang Yoto's successor, Dr. Hj. Anna Muawanah, M.H., in 2019. This bridge has a length of 145 meters and a width of 9 meters.

Lettu Soejitno Statue

First Lieutenant Suyitno or First Lieutenant (posthumous) Raden Mas Soejitno Koesoemobroto is a National Hero of Bojonegoro. The statue was built in the Bojonegoro district square.

Khayangan Api

Khayangan Api is a source of eternal fire that never goes out located in a protected forest area in Sendangharjo Village, Ngasem District, Bojonegoro Regency, East Java.

The Kayangan Api Complex is a natural geological phenomenon in the form of natural gas being released from the ground which is ignited by fire, creating a fire that never goes out even when it rains.

Negeri Atas Angin

Atas Angin is a tourist location in the form of a hill located in Deling Village, Sekar District, Bojonegoro Regency, East Java. There you can see the expanse of natural beauty. You can also enjoy the sunrise view there.
